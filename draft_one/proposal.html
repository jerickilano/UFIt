Workout App

Problem Description

Our area of interest is the general exercise and fitness space. Exercise is very important for human health, however many people tend to skip exercising daily due to the inconvenience it may cause to fit in a workout into their daily routine. We will seek to address this issue by first discovering why exactly people tend to find working out inconveneient. This happens for a number of reasons. Some of the issues we have found is that:

  • People may not know where to begin if they are new to working out
  • People may need motivation to workout consistently
  • People may get tired of their workouts and need new things to try

This leads to the main information technology problem within this space. There is currently no platform that allows for users to learn about workouts based on their personal goals, while accounting for all types of users whether they are just beginning to workout or are more advanced and are looking for new workouts.

There have been many applications created to address this issue for those looking to workout. One example is Fitness Point. The main issue with these past attempts was that these applications were tailored towards specific types of workouts or specific people instead of being general and useful to those looking to try different types of workouts. Although these applications provide feautres such as the ability to log workouts or find new exercises, they are not created for all levels of experience with working out, and they contain content specific for a type of workout, such as running or lifting, rather than containing all types of exercises.

App Description

Everybody is welcome to use our application. Our application is aiming to serve trainers inclusively with their diverse fitness needs. No matter you are a college student, model, or the disabled, as long as you have the needs for workout, our application is here to motivate you and help you grinding. The users can customize their own workout plan through our application. Some users may focus on losing weight, then our application may suggest more cardio exercises. Some users may want to gain muscle, then our application will promote more strength trainings. By accessing our application, the users can also build up flexible schedule. Many people may have tight schedule or don't have the access to fitness equipments (Check this news for more information about our workout environment). Our application also provides numerous unarmed exercise plan to make workout happen in anytime anywhere.

Our application feautres:

  1. Our application provides two kinds of workouts. The users can either create an individual workout or set up a whole workout plan. After they create a workout, our application provide a filter function called "By body part". The users then can choose which body parts they want to work on. And under each body parts, our application will have a list of exercises for the users to add to their workout plan.
  2. Our application will provide the user with a functional timer. For exercises such as running, jogging, and other cardiovascular exercises, a timer will be available once the user selects these specific workouts. The user will then be prompted with an input box to enter the desired countdown time (in seconds) for the timer.
  3. Our application will allow users to give us feedbacks and reviews in our review section. These reviews include the users' experiences, suggestiions, and all things users can think of to help us get better. And other users are also able to view these reviews to get a better sense about our application or get some tips about their workouts. For some experienced fitness enthusiast, the review section also provide a platform for them to create professional workout plan for other users to refer to.

The users will mainly view the information about the exercise. Our application is user-friendly not only for the hardcore trainer but also the beginner. Our plan generator follows a very scientific pattern. Human health is what we care about.
